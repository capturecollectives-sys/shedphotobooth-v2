# 📷 IMAGES — What to Add

Drop your images into this `images/` folder.
Name them exactly as listed below and the site will pick them up automatically.

---

## REQUIRED (site won't look right without these)

| Filename              | Where used              | What it should be                                      | Ideal size     |
|-----------------------|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| logo-white.png        | Nav + Footer            | Your logo, white version, transparent background       | ~400px wide    |
| hero-bg.jpg           | Homepage hero           | Wide atmospheric shot — booth at event, festival crowd | 1920 x 1080px  |

## TIPS

- All photos should be JPG or PNG
- Compress images before uploading (use squoosh.app — free and easy)
- Landscape photos work best for most slots
- Min 800px wide for any gallery image
- Hero background should be at least 1920px wide

---

## LOGO EXPORT TIPS

From your logo file, export two versions:
1. `logo-white.png` — white version, transparent background (for nav/footer on dark backgrounds)
2. `logo-dark.png` — dark/colour version (for light backgrounds if needed later)
